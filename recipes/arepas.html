Best Arepa recipe on internet

Description

Traditional cornmeal patties that are crispy on the outside, soft inside, and often split open and filled with cheese, meat, or eggs.

Ingredients

  • Pre-cooked corn flour
  • Warm water
  • Salt
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. Mix corn flour, salt, and water into a soft dough.
  2. Shape into thick patties.
  3. Cook on a hot pan with oil, ~5 minutes per side.
  4. Split and fill if desired (cheese, meat, eggs).
