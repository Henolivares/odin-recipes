Best Lasagna recipe on internet

Description

A classic baked pasta dish made by layering noodles with savory tomato sauce, seasoned meat or cheese, and melted mozzarella until rich and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or ricotta/vegetables)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta or béchamel
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Cook noodles until al dente.
  2. Brown beef, season, add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for ~40 minutes.
